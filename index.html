How to Play


Objective

Get to the end of the board without dying! Or, capture three Jacks, which lets you safely leave his basement.

Turns

You and the Jacks take turns moving. During your turn, you can roll a dice to move. During the Jacks' turn, the Jacks randomly move forward or backwards one space.

Debuffs

The board is riddled with certain debuffs. Landing on them will affect your next dice roll, or move you around the board.

Capture Jacks

You can capture a Jack by landing on a square with a Jack on it.

Dying

If a Jack lands on you, the game is over UNLESS you have captive Jacks. If so, the captive Jack and the Jack on the board fight to the death and you scamper off unhurt.
