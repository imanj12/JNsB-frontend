How to Play

Objective: Get to the end of the board without dying.

Turns: Players and Jacks take turns moving. During the player turn, the player can roll a dice to move. During Jacks' turn, the Jacks move forward or backwards one space.

Debuffs: The board is riddled with certain debuffs. Landing on them will affect the character's next dice roll, or move the character around the board.

Capture Jacks: Players can capture a Jack by landing on a square with a Jack on it.

Dying: If a Jack lands on a player, the game is over UNLESS the player has captive Jacks. If so, the captive Jack and the Jack on the board fight to the death and you scamper off unhurt.
